Organize a Tour

Feel free to go on The Path anytime. The Pemberton Mill is accessible via a pedestrian bridge. But if you want to go inside the buildings, read on.

The two historical organizations on the tour are open every weekday from 9am-4pm.

The Lawrence Heritage State Park Visitor’s Center is also open over the weekend, same hours, and has free admission. Call ahead to see if there is any special programming available: (978) 794-1655.

If you’d like to check out the vault inside the Lawrence History Center, call ahead at (978) 686-9230. Unlike the Visitor’s Center, this non-profit archive relies on contributions from viewers like you.

The Everett Mills business center is open 7am-5pm daily. Over the weekends you may enter the building through the back entrance. The phone number for the main office is (978) 682-9463.
